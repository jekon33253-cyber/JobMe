# Design System: JobMe

## Brand Personality
Modern, clean, approachable, trustworthy

## Audience
- Candidates (seeking transparent legal work, upskilling, and no hidden fees)
- Employers (seeking fast hiring, low turnover, and zero bureaucracy through a network of Freecruiters)

## Colors
- **Primary CTA / Brand:** Lime Green (`#8CC63F`)
- **Secondary / Accents:** Teal / Cyan (`#00A99D`)
- **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Section Contrast:** Light Gray (`#F9FAFB`)
- **Text:** Graphite (`#333333`)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, rounded modern sans-serif
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif

## Shapes & Geometry
- Use rounded corners (`rounded-lg` or `rounded-xl`) for buttons and cards. This matches the soft, rounded typography of the JobMe logo.

## Language
- Polish

---

### Section 6: Design System Notes for Stitch Generation
When generating pages, apply the following design rules:
1. Use a clean, trustworthy aesthetic with `#FFFFFF` background and `#F9FAFB` for alternating sections.
2. Ensure high contrast for text using `#333333` (Graphite).
3. Primary action buttons should be Lime Green (`#8CC63F`) with rounded-full or rounded-xl corners.
4. Secondary elements, outlines, or gradients should incorporate Teal/Cyan (`#00A99D`).
5. Fonts must be a modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Nunito, or similar rounded font for headings).
6. Cards and containers must use soft shadows and large border radii (`rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`).
